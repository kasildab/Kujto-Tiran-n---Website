Get to know Tirana better 🗎

Tirana is more than just the capital of Albania—it is the heart of the country's history, transformation, and collective memory. A city that has witnessed decades of change, resilience, and cultural evolution, Tirana stands as a symbol of progress and identity.

Through Kujto Tiranën, we chose Tirana as the home for our time capsule because it encapsulates the spirit of an entire nation. From its layered history to its rapid modernization, every street, building, and story holds a piece of Albania's past, present, and future. By preserving memories here, we honor the experiences of generations, ensuring that the essence of Tirana is never forgotten.

This city is where personal and collective narratives intertwine—where history meets the future. That's why Tirana.

Preserving Tirana's History, One Memory at a Time

Kujto Tiranën is a digital time capsule designed to preserve and celebrate the evolving history of Tirana. Through an interactive map, users can explore how different locations have changed over the years, read and share stories, and contribute their own experiences.

Understanding Tirana's past helps shape its future. By preserving historical narratives, we strengthen our cultural identity, foster a deeper appreciation for the city's transformation, and ensure that future generations can connect with its history.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To create a digital time capsule where users can explore and contribute to Tirana's evolving history.

Our Vision

A community-driven platform where memories, places, and events are preserved for future generations.
